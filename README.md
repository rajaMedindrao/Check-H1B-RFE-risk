# H-1B RFE Risk Calculator

**Live demo → [rajamedindrao.github.io/Check-H1B-RFE-risk](https://rajamedindrao.github.io/Check-H1B-RFE-risk/)**

A pre-filing risk scorer for H-1B petitions. Answer 18 questions, get a 0–100 risk score with flagged weak points and specific remediation steps — in under three minutes.

---

## What it does

Most H-1B RFEs cluster around a handful of predictable triggers: specialty occupation weakness, wage-level contradictions, degree-to-role mismatches, and worksite structure issues. This tool scores petitions across those five categories before filing — surfacing the exact flags attorneys would catch, so you can fix them first.

- **Real-time scoring** across 5 weighted categories (Specialty Occupation, Wage Level, Degree Alignment, Worksite, Employer History)
- **Compound risk detection** — catches dangerous combinations like Level 1 wage + 6+ years of experience
- **Plain-English flags** with specific remediation steps for each issue
- No signup, no backend — runs entirely in the browser
